Welcome to PITT STOP

Pitt Stop is a community-built hub designed to support healthier, happier lives for the people of Pitt County and nearby areas. By bringing together healthcare providers, educators, organizations, schools, colleges and university systems, agencies, healthcare systems, and businesses, Pitt Stop makes it easier than ever to access the services you need - all in one welcoming space.

At Pitt Stop, you'll find care, resources, and opportunities delivered side by side, because we believe everyone can live their best community life, built together, through trusted and lasting partnerships.

Pitt Stop

Pitt Stop was born from a shared vision: to offer a sustainable way for Pitt County organizations, schools, college and university systems, agencies, healthcare systems, and businesses to connect and work together to improve the overall physical, mental, emotional health, well-being, and quality of life for our residents. Local leaders, organizations, and citizens created the Pitt Stop to provide a physical space for connection and collaboration with the community. Every detail of Pitt Stop reflects this commitment - built by the community for the community.

History of the Pitt Stop Building

The Pitt Stop building has a story of its own. In recent years, this building housed the Bethel Police Department. However for many years, the Pitt Stop building was the home of the Bethel Clinic. Today, the building serves a new purpose, building now serves as a welcoming space for healthcare, education, and community services, making it easier for families and individuals to find support close to home.
