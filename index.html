Welcome To PITT STOP

Pitt Stop is a community-built hub designed to support healthier, happier lives for the people of Pitt County and nearby areas. By bringing together healthcare providers, educators, employment services, and community partners, Pitt Stop makes it easier than ever to access the services you need - all in one welcoming space.

At Pitt Stop, you’ll find care, resources, and opportunities delivered side by side, because we believe a stronger, healthier community is built together through trusted and caring partnerships.

Pitt Stop

Pitt Stop was born from a shared vision: to offer a sustainable way for Pitt County organizations, schools, college and university systems, agencies,healthcare systems, and community members to work together to deliver a wide range of services that improve the health, well-being, and quality of life for our residents. Local leaders, organizations, and residents made a commitment to design a center that listens to, responds to, and grows with the community. Every detail of Pitt Stop reflects this commitment - built by the community, for the community.

History of the Pitt Stop Building

The Pitt Stop building has a story of its own. In recent years, this building hosted the Bethel Police Department. However for many years, the Pitt Stop building was the home of the Bethel Clinic. Mayor Carl Wilson was born in this building! Renovated with care and purpose, this building now serves as a welcoming space for healthcare, education, and community services, making it easier for families and individuals to find support close to home.
